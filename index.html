What Combining Actually Changes
  • One authority pool. Every backlink from the Authority Building program, every new page, every citation compounds under one domain instead of being split across two AS-9-and-under sites.
  • No more duplicate content. The 13 shared posts (and the broader topic overlap — cybersecurity, NVR/DVR, cloud recording) get written once and served to both markets, with each optimized instead of competing with its own twin.
  • Future markets slot in for free. Fort Worth, San Antonio, or any future city becomes a new /locations/ page on existing infrastructure — not a third domain, a third retainer, and a third uphill authority climb.
  • Google Business Profiles don't move.
